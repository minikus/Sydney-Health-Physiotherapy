Acupuncture: how it works?
Office workers who works long stressful hours may have experienced insomnia, anxiety, and Upset Stomach. Whenever there is work-life imbalance, energy flow is disrupted. Here in Sydney Health Physio CBD, we integrate acupuncture with physiotherapy.

Acupuncture views bodily health through the prism of energy (Qi) movement. If the energy flow is disrupted, bodily imbalance occurs. Acupuncture aims to rebalance our body energy flow, improve sleep and lower stress level. Energy flow and vitality can be enhanced through acupuncture, to promote inner motivation for exercises, enthusiasm, and to ultimately prevent "burn-out". Soft tissue quality can be improved through integrated needling techniques. Sydney Health Physio CBD provides treatment via massage, manual therapy, and integrated needling to assist the body to rebalance and assume better health. This is particularly important in today’s world where everything is moving forward at a fast pace. A balanced energy flow in harmony is a must to ensure life’s longevity and wellbeing.
