The Needs and Benefits of Physiotherapy

The condition of musculoskeletal pain is often due to flare-ups of local tissues, and the pain often persists as a result of compensatory movements. Physiotherapy is specialised to settle down painful tissue and restore optimal body movements.

Here at Sydney Health Physiotherapy, we are in helping you gain back your optimal movement as well as your health back. We are professionals for lower back pain, arthritis, heart disease, fractures, head injuries, pre and post-surgical procedures. Systematic physical rehabilitation can help restore mobility, provide relief to pain and limit permanent physical disabilities.

Meredith Chapple

Physiotherapist

Meredith is a graduate from the Doctor of Physiotherapy program at Macquarie University. She has worked with several Rugby Union clubs and is skilled in managing acute injuries, return to sport programs, and pre-season screening. Working with athletes has helped her empower all patients to return to their activities of choice in their best capacity.

Meredith as also taught Yoga and Pilates which has helped developed keen body awareness and skill in incorporating movement, mindfulness and functional exercises as part of all rehabilitative programs. She is passionate about the connections in the body and has pursued on-going clinical education in dry needling, functional movement, exercise rehab, women’s health and TMJ dysfunction.

Clinically, she enjoys working patients experiencing chronic pain, low back pain, and those recovering from sporting injury. She works with patients to educate them, develop a suitable exercise program and use manual therapy techniques for the best results. Outside of the clinic Meredith has a passion for running, rock climbing, yoga and playing ukulele.
